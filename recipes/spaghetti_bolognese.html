Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Treat yourself to the classic and comforting flavors of Spaghetti Bolognese. This beloved Italian dish features al dente spaghetti noodles tossed in a robust and savory meat sauce. The sauce is made by simmering a combination of ground beef, onions, garlic, and tomatoes, along with a blend of herbs and spices, creating a deeply flavorful and hearty accompaniment to the pasta. Top it off with a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese and fresh herbs for a delightful finishing touch.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Onion, finely chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Beef or vegetable broth
  • Red wine (optional)
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Olive oil
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving
  • Fresh basil or parsley, for garnish

How to make Spaghetti Bolognese

  1. In a large pot, cook the spaghetti noodles according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a separate large skillet or saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, saut√©ing until they turn translucent and fragrant.
  3. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned, breaking it up into smaller pieces with a wooden spoon.
  4. Stir in the Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper, adjusting the amounts to your taste. Mix well to incorporate the flavors.
  5. Add the canned tomatoes, tomato paste, beef or vegetable broth, and red wine (if using). Stir well to combine all the ingredients.
  6. Reduce the heat to low and let the sauce simmer uncovered for about 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together and the sauce to thicken. Stir occasionally.
  7. Taste the sauce and adjust the seasonings as needed. Add more salt, pepper, or Italian seasoning if desired.
  8. Serve the cooked spaghetti noodles with a generous ladle of the Bolognese sauce on top. Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil or parsley.
  9. Toss the spaghetti and sauce together until well combined, ensuring that the noodles are evenly coated with the sauce.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy the rich and comforting flavors of Spaghetti Bolognese.
