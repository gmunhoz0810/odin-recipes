Chicken Alfredo

Description

Indulge in the creamy and comforting flavors of Chicken Alfredo. This Italian-inspired dish features tender chicken breast pieces smothered in a velvety and rich Alfredo sauce. The sauce is made with butter, cream, garlic, and Parmesan cheese, resulting in a luscious and satisfying flavor. Serve it over al dente fettuccine noodles for a delightful pasta dish that will impress your taste buds. Top it off with a sprinkle of fresh parsley for a touch of freshness.

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine noodles
  • Chicken breast, boneless and skinless
  • Butter
  • Heavy cream
  • Garlic, minced
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

How to make Chicken Alfredo

  1. Cook the fettuccine noodles according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chicken breast pieces and cook until they are no longer pink in the center.
  3. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside. In the same skillet, melt the butter over medium heat.
  4. Add the minced garlic to the skillet and saut√© until fragrant, being careful not to burn it.
  5. Pour the heavy cream into the skillet, stirring constantly to combine it with the melted butter and garlic.
  6. Gradually add the grated Parmesan cheese to the skillet, stirring continuously until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
  7. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste, adjusting the amounts according to your preference.
  8. Slice the cooked chicken breast into thin strips and add them to the skillet, stirring gently to coat them with the Alfredo sauce.
  9. Add the cooked fettuccine noodles to the skillet, tossing them with the sauce and chicken until well combined.
  10. Serve the Chicken Alfredo hot, garnished with fresh parsley for a vibrant touch.
